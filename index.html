☀ Daytime

Day Theme

When golden light spills across the horizon and the sky stretches out in endless blue, the world wakes up bright and full of promise. Warm breezes carry the scent of morning, and every colour blazes vivid under the generous sun.

✦ Nighttime

Night Theme

As darkness drapes the sky, a thousand ancient stars emerge to trace their silver stories. The world grows hushed, the moon pours soft light across the stillness, and the night wraps around you like a quiet, infinite calm.
